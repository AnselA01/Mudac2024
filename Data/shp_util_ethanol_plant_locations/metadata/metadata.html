Section 1 Overview
Originator Minnesota Department of Agriculture
Title Ethanol Plants, Minnesota
Abstract Locations of commercial ethanol manufacturing plants in the state of Minnesota as identified to the Department of Agriculture.
Purpose This data set was developed with the intent of enabling GIS professionals, regulatory personnel, and emergency responders with accurate information regarding the locations of ethanol plants in Minnesota. This is considered industrial information and is not consumer oriented since the products dispensed require bulk purchases and/or licensed users.

3Medium: Location has been matched to a Public Land Survey (PLS) description to the section level or beyond
4Low: Location has been matched to a Public Land Survey (PLS) description at the township level, or a zip code, city or county centroid
